Responsible Disclosure Policy – Philemon Samuel Portfolio

Last updated: March 29, 2026

I take the security of my portfolio site seriously. If you’ve discovered a security vulnerability, I appreciate your help disclosing it to me privately so I can verify and address it before any public disclosure.

Scope

  • https://philportifolio.netlify.app/ and all its subpages
  • Any static or dynamic content hosted under this domain (including contact forms, if any)
Out of scope:
  • Third-party services embedded (e.g., GitHub Gists, YouTube videos) – please report those to the respective vendor.
  • Social engineering, physical attacks, or DDoS testing.

Guidelines

  • Do not access, modify, or delete data that isn’t your own.
  • Do not use automated scanners that generate high traffic without prior notice.
  • Do not share the vulnerability publicly before I’ve had a chance to fix it.
  • Provide clear steps to reproduce the issue (screenshots or PoC code are welcome).

How to Report

Send an email to: security@philportifolio.netlify.app or simply click this link Contact Me

  • A descriptive subject line (e.g., “Security issue on portfolio contact page”)
  • Affected URL
  • Type of issue (XSS, CSP bypass, insecure redirect, etc.)
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Potential impact

I will acknowledge receipt within 3 business days.

What to Expect

  • I will investigate and confirm the vulnerability.
  • I will work to fix it as soon as possible (typically within 7–14 days).
  • I will not take legal action against you if you follow this policy.
  • I will credit you on this page (with your consent) after the fix is deployed.

Safe Harbor

Any act performed in good faith according to this policy is considered authorized, and I will not pursue legal action against you.

Thank you for helping keep this small corner of the web secure.
